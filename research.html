Working Paper

Delivering Degrees: Positive Educational Spillovers from Amazon's Logistics Expansion Working paper. [pdf copy]

  • This paper examines how Amazon fulfillment center expansion affects local educational attainment across U.S. commuting zones between 2010 and 2023.
  • The results suggest positive effects on bachelor's and master's degree attainment through changes in returns to schooling, technology spillovers, and selective in-migration.
  • Submitted to the Society of Labor Economists (SOLE) Conference.
