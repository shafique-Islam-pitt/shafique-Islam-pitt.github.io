Welcome!

I am a third-year PhD student in Economics at the University of Pittsburgh.

My research interests are in labor economics, development economics, public economics, and applied econometrics.

Before starting my PhD, I completed an MA in Economics at Central Michigan University and earned both my MSS and BSS in Economics from the University of Dhaka.

My work combines policy-oriented economic questions with empirical analysis using large-scale datasets, text data, and time-series methods.

Feel free to reach out to me at shafique.islam@pitt.edu.
