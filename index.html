About Me

I am a third-year PhD student in Economics at the University of Pittsburgh. My research focuses on labor economics, development economics, public economics, and applied econometrics.

Before starting my PhD, I completed an MA in Economics at Central Michigan University and earned both my MSS and BSS in Economics from the University of Dhaka. My broader work combines policy-oriented economic questions with empirical analysis using large-scale datasets, text data, and time-series methods.
