About

Economics, policy, and applied research

Working Papers

Delivering Degrees: Positive Educational Spillovers from Amazon's Logistics Expansion

This paper studies how Amazon fulfillment center expansion affects local educational attainment across U.S. commuting zones between 2010 and 2023. The findings suggest positive effects on bachelor's and master's degree attainment through changes in returns to schooling, technology spillovers, and selective in-migration.

This paper was submitted to the Society of Labor Economists (SOLE) Conference.
